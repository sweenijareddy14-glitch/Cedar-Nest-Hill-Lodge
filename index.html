Why stay with us

Cedar Nest Hill Lodge in Manali Himachal

We keep things simple so more of your budget goes toward the mountains, not the marble lobby. Enjoy pristine valley scenery and direct trail access right outside our doors.

Our budget hotel properties offer a unique mix of simple luxury standard rooms, private balcony family accommodations, and highly affordable backpacker dorm options tailored exactly to your travel needs. Experience authentic local hospitality at our lodge, sit under the starry sky by the evening bonfire, or grab a warm beverage at our home-style rooftop terrace cafe. Every cozy corner of our property is fully optimized to provide deep rest, security, and mountain comfort for adventure hikers, families, and solo backpackers exploring the legendary wilderness trails and peaks of the Pir Panjal mountain ranges.
